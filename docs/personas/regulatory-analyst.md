# Persona: Regulatory Analyst

**Primary User**

## Demographics
- **Experience**: 2-5 years in compliance or regulatory roles
- **Education**: Law degree, compliance certification, or financial services background
- **Role**: Junior to mid-level analyst responsible for analyzing specific regulatory areas or business lines
- **Reporting**: Reports to Compliance Manager or Head of Compliance

## Goals
- Quickly assess whether a new regulation impacts assigned business lines
- Complete initial impact assessments accurately and efficiently
- Find relevant prior regulations to ensure consistent interpretation
- Document analysis with proper source citations for audit trail
- Reduce time spent on manual document review and research

## Pain Points
- Time pressure: Tight deadlines (30-90 days from publication to enforcement)
- Information fragmentation: Must consult multiple sources (regulatory websites, PDFs, newsletters)
- Difficulty finding related regulations and prior versions
- Knowledge gaps: Limited access to institutional regulatory history
- Unclear priorities: Difficulty determining what regulations most impact their assigned area

## Technology Comfort
- Proficient with Google, Excel, PDF readers, standard office tools
- Familiar with regulatory databases (Bloomberg Law, Westlaw, proprietary tools)
- Learning curve acceptable if tool reduces overall time investment

## Success Criteria
- Can complete initial impact assessment in hours, not days
- Confidence in finding all relevant regulatory sources
- Easy access to prior regulations and amendment history
- Clear citation trail for audit and stakeholder communication

## Typical Workflow
1. Receives notification of new regulation
2. Reads executive summary and rule text (30-60 minutes)
3. Searches for related prior regulations (1-2 hours currently)
4. Drafts preliminary impact assessment (2-4 hours)
5. Shares with Compliance Manager for review

## Key Needs from RegAI Copilot
- Semantic search to find regulations by concept, not just keyword
- Organized retrieval of regulation versions and related rules
- Source citations for audit trail
- Structured output to organize obligations by business line
- Fast turnaround (minutes, not hours) on initial retrieval phase
