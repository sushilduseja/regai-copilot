# Persona: Compliance Manager

**Secondary User / Quality Gate**

## Demographics
- **Experience**: 10+ years in compliance, legal, or risk management
- **Education**: Law degree or advanced business degree common
- **Role**: Manager or director responsible for oversight of multiple analysts and regulatory coverage areas
- **Reporting**: Reports to Chief Compliance Officer or Head of Risk

## Goals
- Ensure comprehensive regulatory coverage across all assigned business lines
- Maintain consistent analysis methodology and quality standards
- Reduce time reviewing analyst work (quality gate function)
- Build institutional knowledge that survives analyst turnover
- Prepare for regulatory examinations with clear documentation
- Allocate resources effectively to highest-impact regulatory changes

## Pain Points
- Inconsistent analysis quality across team members
- Knowledge silos: Expertise concentrated in few individuals
- Time-intensive review of analyst work (need to verify completeness)
- Staff turnover creates risk: Regulatory expertise leaves organization
- Difficult to scale team without proportional quality risks
- Manual processes don't scale; difficult to manage large volume of regulatory changes

## Technology Comfort
- Comfortable with enterprise systems, dashboards, reporting tools
- May have analytics or business intelligence experience
- Values operational efficiency and risk reduction
- Requires clear ROI for tool investments

## Success Criteria
- Can quickly review and validate analyst work
- Confident nothing material was missed in initial assessment
- Reduced analysis time across team (20%+ efficiency gain)
- Better knowledge retention and onboarding of new analysts
- Comprehensive audit trail for regulatory examinations

## Typical Workflow
1. Receives analyst preliminary assessment
2. Verifies completeness (re-reads regulation, checks for related rules) (2-4 hours)
3. Identifies gaps or inconsistencies with prior interpretations (1-2 hours)
4. Reviews quality and documentation (1 hour)
5. Escalates findings and provides feedback to analyst

## Key Needs from RegAI Copilot
- Consistent, reproducible analysis across analyst team
- Quick ability to verify analyst conclusions against source
- Centralized regulatory knowledge base to improve team onboarding
- Metrics on coverage and consistency
- Audit-ready documentation of analysis process
- Change history and amendment tracking to avoid missed updates
