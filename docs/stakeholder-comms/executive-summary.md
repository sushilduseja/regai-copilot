## Executive Summary (1-Pager)

**The Problem:** Capital markets firms are struggling to keep pace with the volume and complexity of financial regulations. Manual analysis is slow, costly, and risks missing critical obligations, leading to potential fines and reputational damage.

**The Solution:** RegAI Copilot is a RAG-powered intelligence system designed for compliance teams. It automates the retrieval and analysis of regulatory documents, enabling teams to assess impacts in hours, not weeks.

**Expected Benefits:**
-   **Increased Efficiency:** Drastically reduce the time spent on manual document review.
-   **Reduced Risk:** Ensure comprehensive and consistent analysis, with a full audit trail.
-   **Enhanced Knowledge:** Centralize regulatory intelligence and make it instantly accessible.

**Resource Requirements:**
-   Dedicated engineering squad (1 PM, 1 Tech Lead, 4 Engineers).
-   Initial 3-month development for MVP.

**Key Risks & Mitigations:**
-   **AI Accuracy:** Mitigated by a human-in-the-loop design and mandatory source citations.
-   **User Adoption:** Mitigated by a phased rollout and a comprehensive change management plan.
