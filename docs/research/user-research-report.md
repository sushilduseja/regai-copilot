## User Research Report

### Research Activities
- Stakeholder interviews with compliance officers, legal counsel, and risk managers.
- Observational research shadowing the regulatory change management process.
- Journey mapping of the current workflow from regulation publication to implementation.
- Pain point prioritization workshops with cross-functional teams.

### Persona Development

**Persona 1: Regulatory Analyst (Primary User)**
- **Background**: 2-5 years experience, law or compliance degree.
- **Goals**: Quickly assess if a new regulation impacts assigned business lines.
- **Pain Points**: Difficulty finding relevant prior regulations, time pressure, unclear priorities.
- **Success Criteria**: Can complete an initial impact assessment in hours, not days.

**Persona 2: Compliance Manager (Secondary User)**
- **Background**: 10+ years experience, oversees multiple analysts.
- **Goals**: Ensure comprehensive coverage, consistent methodology, and audit readiness.
- **Pain Points**: Inconsistent analysis quality, knowledge retention when analysts leave.
- **Success Criteria**: Can review analyst work quickly, confident nothing was missed.

**Persona 3: Technology/Operations Lead (Tertiary User)**
- **Background**: Responsible for implementing regulatory changes in systems.
- **Goals**: Understand technical requirements and estimate implementation effort.
- **Pain Points**: Late notification of changes, vague requirements.
- **Success Criteria**: Receives clear technical requirements with sufficient lead time.

### Jobs-to-be-Done Framework
- "When a new regulation is published, I need to understand if it applies to my business so I can prioritize my work."
- "When assessing impact, I need to find related regulations so I can ensure consistent interpretation."
- "When documenting analysis, I need to cite specific regulatory text so I can support my conclusions in audits."
